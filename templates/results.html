How Zero-Knowledge Proof (ZKP) Ensures Privacy:

This voting system uses encryption to keep each vote private. Votes are encrypted immediately and verified without revealing their contents.

The results are tallied securely, and only the final count is revealed to maintain voter anonymity.

All encryption and verification happen behind the scenes, ensuring that the integrity of each vote is preserved without compromising privacy.
