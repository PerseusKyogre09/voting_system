Behind the Scenes: Zero-Knowledge Proof (ZKP)

To protect voter privacy, each vote is encrypted using secure cryptography. Our system verifies votes without accessing the content, ensuring both privacy and accuracy.

Zero-Knowledge Proof (ZKP) guarantees that the tally process is both secure and transparent, preserving anonymity while confirming authenticity.
